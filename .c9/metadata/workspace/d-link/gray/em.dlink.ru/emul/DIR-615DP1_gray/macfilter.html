\t\t\t\t\t The MAC address of a device from the router's LAN. You can enter the MAC address of a device connected to the router's LAN at the moment. To do this, select the relevant device from the Known IP/MAC addresses вdrop-down list (the field will be filled in automatically). \t\t\t\t

\t\t\t\t

\t\t\t\t

\t\t\t\t\tAllow: Allows access to the router's network and to the Internet for the device with the specified MAC when the rules on the Firewall / IP filters page block access for this device. \t\t\t
